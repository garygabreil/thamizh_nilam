Cancellation and Refund Policy

1. General Policy

At Thamizh Nilam, we understand that plans can change. Our cancellation and refund policy is designed to be fair to both our visitors and our small farm business.

2. Cancellation Timeframes

Cancellation Period Refund Amount
More than 7 days before visit 100% refund
3-7 days before visit 50% refund
Less than 72 hours before visit No refund
No-show No refund

3. How to Cancel

To cancel your booking, please contact us via:

  • Phone: +91 98765 43210 (during business hours)
  • Email: bookings@thamizhnilam.com
  • WhatsApp: +91 98765 43210

Cancellations are only valid when confirmed by our staff.

4. Refund Processing

a. Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days.
b. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.
c. Bank processing times may vary depending on your financial institution.

5. Weather Cancellations

In case of severe weather conditions that make farm visits unsafe, we may cancel your booking. You will have the option to:

  • Reschedule your visit (subject to availability)
  • Receive a full refund

6. Special Events

For workshops, festivals, and special events, different cancellation policies may apply. These will be clearly stated at time of booking.
