Shipping and Delivery Policy

1. Processing Time

All orders are processed within 2-3 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.

2. Domestic Shipping

Shipping Method Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 5-7 business days ₹50-100 (based on weight)
Express Shipping 2-3 business days ₹150-250 (based on weight)

Note: Delivery times are estimates and not guaranteed. Rural areas may experience longer delivery times.

3. International Shipping

We currently do not offer international shipping for our farm products due to agricultural restrictions.

4. Local Pickup

You may pick up your order at our farm during business hours. After placing your order, you will receive an email with pickup instructions. Please bring your order confirmation email and valid ID.

Pickup Address:
Thamizh Nilam Organic Farm
Near Mamallapuram, Tamil Nadu, India

5. Damaged or Lost Packages

If your package arrives damaged or you have not received it within the expected timeframe:

  • Contact us immediately at support@thamizhnilam.com
  • Provide your order number and photos of damaged items
  • We will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue

Claims must be made within 3 days of delivery.

6. Perishable Items

Some of our fresh products are perishable. We take special care in packaging these items with ice packs and insulated packaging during warmer months. However, we cannot guarantee the condition of perishable items if delivery is delayed due to carrier issues.
